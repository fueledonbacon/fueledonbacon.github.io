Prentice Law

Scope of Work

Prentice Law is an estate and elder law firm based in Melbourne, FL. Alec K. Prentice, founder and president of Prentice Law expressed interest in having a new website built in order to garner a better online presence. He wanted something that would showcase all their practice areas in an easy to navigate way, while also increasing their visibility in online searches.

Project Results

After meeting with Alec Prentice, we learned that his practice needed a fresh take on branding. To go along with a new, optimized website build, we also provided Prentice Law with custom stock photography services, and custom copy for each of the practices they offer. On top of this we designed a new logo, and had business cards made for their office. When everything was said and done, Prentice Law received a new brand, a brand launch, and a new website. Prentice Law now ranks on the first page of Google for Elder Law in Melbourne Florida.
